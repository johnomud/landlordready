A fixed-term tenancy has a specific end date written into the agreement (e.g. a 12-month contract). A periodic tenancy rolls on month-by-month with no end date.

How do you currently increase rent?
✓Informally / verbally
Formal written notice

From 1 May 2026, all rent increases must go through the formal Section 13 process using the prescribed Form 4A — verbal or informal increases become unlawful.

Do you collect more than 1 month's rent in advance on new tenancies?

From 1 May 2026, collecting more than 1 month's rent in advance on new tenancies is banned. Any excess charged must be refunded.

Section 21 'no-fault' evictions are abolished from 1 May 2026. The last date a valid Section 21 notice can be served is 30 April 2026.
